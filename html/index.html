Brand manifesto

Have you ever finished an article and said, "I would not be able to talk about it"? Feeling frustrated at having spent countless hours reading articles to learn, ultimately, not being able to share with your family, your friends, the world around you the incredible discoveries what did your reading bring you?

What we propose is a simple solution to allow you to store all this knowledge often lost. The story of "Wizer" begins when its creators understand the usefulness of having the opportunity to read an article while having the choice to keep the passages that have the most marked or allow them to have a more conscious reading and to stimulate the intellect as much as possible during reading, all in one application.

One of the creators was in the mountains. Looking at the sky, he saw an eagle fly and thought about this project. Seeing this eagle, it was the revelation. That day, a feeling appeared, the feeling to have free knowledge, to have the choice to retain what he reads, to enrich himself and become free himself in the manner of this volatile.
